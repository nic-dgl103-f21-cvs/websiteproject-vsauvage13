Resort Amenities

Kayaking

Single & Tandem Kayaks are available for day-use free of charge. Explore Harrison Lake from your boat and get up close and personal with local wildlife.

Jewelry Making

Create unique jewelry with locally mined gemstones alongside Marcella in this afternoon class held in paradise. Leave with a dozen completed projects.

Hiking

Hike through 2400 acres of untrailed and wild nature. Visit Mermaid Lake and Dragonfly Lake, a 25 and 45 minute hike from the resort.

Finnish Sauna

A traditional Finnish Sauna House, also called a called a kiuas , made from Birch wood, heated by a wood-burning stove, and with a kiuaskivet for steam.

Reiki

An energy healing treatment that involves using gentle hand movements to guide the flow of healthy energy through the body to reduce stress and anxiety and promote healing.

Crystal & Sound Healings

Pure, high frequency sounds are used to resonate with your physical, emotional and energetic bodies. This aids with cleansing and balancing at the cellular level.

Yoga Classes

Our classes focus on strength, flexibility and breathing to boost physical and mental wellbeing. Varying series of movements designed to increase strength and flexibility.

Chartered Fishing Tours

Freshwater Sturgeon, Steelhead and Salmon half-day and full-day tours are available. No experience is required: our Fishermen are fully certified and have 10 years experience.
