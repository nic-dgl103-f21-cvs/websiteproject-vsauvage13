Our Story

Marcella and Ray started with just an idea and a desire. They both owned a large piece of property on Harrison Lake and decided that they couldn't just keep it to themselves. They wanted to share their piece of paradise with others to enjoy.

They started working on the Elfinlau Resort at the beginning of 2003, converting a corner of their private island to the eco-resort that they had always dreamed of. After 3 years of travelling back and forth to Echo Island, building and planning, they permanently moved to their near completed resort. They continued to work on the property, adding new amenities every couple of months. The most recent of these include the off-the-grid Finnish Sauna House, and the Dreamweaver Studio used for a multitude of different classes, like jewelry making and yoga.

They opened their doors to the public in late 2006. Since then, they have had people from all over the world stay at Elfinlau. Marcella and Ray both feel incredibly privileged to share their private piece of paradise with others.

Meet Your Hosts

Marcella

Marcella was born and raised in the Harrison Lake area. She prides herself on knowing the local land and its inhabitants well. Marcella became quickly certified in many practices including becoming a certified Reiki Master and Yoga Instructor. She has always had a passion for hosting and designed Elfinlau with this at the forefront of her mind.

Ray

Ray moved to Canada from Finland in 1993 and met Marcella shortly after. Ray worked as a Carpenter for 2 decades before becoming a General Contracter for 8 years. Ray brought his professional knowledge to the complete construction of Elfinlau, building the cabins and the sauna from scratch, keeping up on the maintenance, and doing renovations.
