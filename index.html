About Us

Welcome to Elfinlau Paradise. We are an off-the-grid eco-resort located on the private Echo Island in beautiful Harrison Lake. Our Island is 2400 acres of untrailed forest just waiting to be explored.

Elfinlau Eco-Resort offers a memorable experience for travellers of all kinds, providing a unique, eco-friendly cabin rental on the remote and private Echo Island. Come as you are and leave feeling refreshed and grounded.
